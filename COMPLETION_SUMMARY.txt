Q: Do I need to change the frontend?
A: No! Everything on frontend works exactly the same.

Q: Will MongoDB still save messages?
A: Yes! Both MongoDB saves AND emails are sent.

Q: Is my password safe?
A: Yes! Uses app password (not main password), stored in .env (not code), not in Git.

Q: What if email fails?
A: Message still saves to DB. Error is logged but doesn't affect user.

Q: How long until email arrives?
A: 1-3 seconds usually. Gmail can be a bit slow sometimes.

Q: Can I use this on production?
A: Yes! Set environment variables in hosting dashboard.

Q: What if someone can't receive my reply?
A: Each email includes their contact email. Reply directly to them.

Q: Can I send to multiple addresses?
A: Yes, can be added in future. Currently one recipient per .env.

Q: Do I need to pay for this?
A: No! Gmail app passwords are free. Nodemailer is free.
